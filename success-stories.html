EKI ENERGY SERVICES LTD.

CHALLENGE

Managing the vast quantum of data and ensuring accuracy were the main issues for EKI with manual calculations, decentralized data, and a lack of granularity in emission reporting.


SOLUTION

WOCE migrated EKI's databases to Carbon Ledger and Carbon Certificate Management platform. This comprehensive solution enabled the capture, calculation, reporting, and offsetting of Scope 1, 2, 3 emissions and facilitates the tracking, reconciliation, utilization, and trading of their carbon credit inventory, along with efficient management of carbon offsetting projects.


IMPACT

The seamless integration of Carbon Ledger and the Certificate Management platform empowered EKI with centralized inventories, streamlined reporting, tracking, trading and comprehensive dashboards.


CONCLUSION

The implementation of Carbon Ledger and Carbon Certificate Management at EKI demonstrates the transformative impact of technology-driven sustainability solutions. WOCE's intervention not only addressed EKI's challenges but also enabled them to embrace sustainability and offer sustainable solutions with accuracy and transparency.
