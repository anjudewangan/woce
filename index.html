Carbon Ledger is a cutting-edge solution designed to revolutionize carbon accounting and reporting. It serves as a comprehensive platform for businesses to measure, monitor, and manage their carbon emissions efficiently. Specifically designed for comprehensive greenhouse gas (GHG) accounting, Carbon Ledger allows organizations to easily calculate their Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 emissions.

Compliance Ready

Stay ahead of regulatory requirements by utilizing Carbon Ledger, which supports various global reporting frameworks such as GHGRP, GRI, TCFD, PCAF, BRSR etc.

Efficient Data Capture

Carbon Ledger excels in digitizing the capture of carbon emissions data, replacing manual processes with automated systems for swift and error-free data collection.

Success Stories

SkyJumper Trampoline Park

In a groundbreaking collaboration, WOCE has joined forces with Skyjumper, a renowned amusement park chain in India, with the ambitious goal of achieving carbon neutrality in their Gurgaon facility by the end of January 2024. This collaboration aims to make all 10 Skyjumper locations in India carbon neutral by mid-2024, helping Skyjumper build Amusement 2.0 version and set an important precedent in the entertainment sector.

An International Commercial Bank

A phenomenal win in banking industry where an international commercial bank looking out for a solution for GHG Accounting. As part of GHG Accounting, they were looking for a solution to calculate “financed emissions (PCAF)”. WOCE joined hands with the client to give them solution to do their GHG Accounting with PCAF solution using Carbon Ledger. The Bank has more than 150 ATMs and branches for which GHG Accounting has been done along with financed emissions form their loans and investments.

India Based Manufacturing Company

Leveraging Carbon Accounting as a Service, WOCE partnered with a leading manufacturing company in India, precisely calculating the carbon footprint of their spark plug production. This strategic initiative provided accurate insights into emissions for every step in their value chain, giving the company accurate data for creating sustainability strategies.

Carbon Neutral Social for Corporates at Kiran's

In a pioneering move, WOCE orchestrated a carbon-neutral social evening at Kiran's, a popular restaurant in Houston. By meticulously calculating the carbon emissions across different categories (like food, drinks and waste generated) and offsetting it with high quality carbon credits, WOCE showcased the transformative power of sustainability in the hospitality sector.
