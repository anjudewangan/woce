Solution Offerings

Our Solutions for Oil & Gas companies include carbon accounting of scope1, scope 2 and scope 3 emissions. These carbon accounting solutions include capturing of various activities across oil and gas value chain and computing of emissions at every step in value chain using our industry vetted algorithms. Carbon offset management solution (COMS) helps O&G companies to manage their carbon offset projects and carbon certificates across various registries

Oil & Gas Value Chain

Carbon Accounting

Capture Scope 1, Scope 2 and Scope 3 emission along entire energy value chain. Identify various business activities across the value chain and measure carbon emissions along the value chain

Trading and Logistics Decisions

Calculation of emissions at the trade level starting from trade capture- to nomination- to movement enabling business to get detailed insight on emissions by product type, transport type which then can enable business to choose alternate emission friendly options to move/trade the product. This enables trading desk to make better trade decisions keeping sustainability goal into account

Carbon Offset Strategies

Trade level emission calculations allows forecasting of carbon emission across portfolios and business units enabling organization to plan carbon offset strategies in advance by purchasing carbon credits at better prices

Carbon Offset Management

Carbon Offset Management System (COMS) enables organizations to manage & reconcile carbon certificates between registries (compliance & voluntary markets) and Trade Capture systems on one unified carbon intelligence platform
