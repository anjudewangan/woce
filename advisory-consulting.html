Solutions for Green Energy

Sustainability Strategy & Transformation

Our team collaborates with clients to define a comprehensive sustainability strategy and roadmap to achieve Net Zero ambitions. This involves conducting a current state assessment to identify emission hotspots. We then formulate a concrete action plan focusing on the 3 R’s – Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, ensuring a year-over-year reduction in emissions.

Regulatory Compliance

Staying compliant with evolving regulations is crucial. Our team keeps clients informed about changing policies and regulatory landscapes. We facilitate tracking and reporting of Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions, helping transition from traditional Excel spreadsheets to the Carbon Ledger Platform. This platform supports various reporting frameworks such as CDP, TCFD, GRI, SECR, GRESB, NGER, BRSR and PCAF.

Supply Chain Sustainability for Industry Sectors

In recent years a rising number of corporations have pledged to work with suppliers that adhere to social and environmental standards. The aim is to create a cascade of sustainable practices. Our team of experts can assist in business operations assessment & waste management assessment across the whole business value chain across industries such as Oil& Gas, Utilities, Manufacturing, Logistics, Finance, Events and Entertainment, Travel and Hospitality and Services.

Carbon/Energy/Commodity Trading

Navigating the complexities of carbon markets and trading systems, our team advises on opportunities in both Compliance and Voluntary carbon markets. We help define carbon pricing strategies and manage the entire carbon credit lifecycle across various registries using the Carbon Certificate Management software, ensuring organizations can participate in and benefit from these markets.

Climate Investments

For banks and insurance companies, managing financial risks associated with climate change is paramount. Our experts guide clients in embedding sustainable investment practices throughout their businesses. As ESG investments gain prominence, we assist in building sustainable portfolios, considering ESG scores as a key factor in investment decisions.

Product Rollout

As organizations accelerate their journey towards net-zero goals, we offer in-house products to facilitate smoother adoption of sustainable business processes. Our products come with trial periods and end-user training to ensure seamless integration and engagement, supporting clients in embracing change effectively.

Change Management & Training

Embracing sustainability requires effective change management and training. Our team creates tailor-made training courses to drive organizational change management, ensuring employees align with the company’s vision. Measurable outcomes are a key focus, ensuring sustainable practices become ingrained in the corporate culture.
