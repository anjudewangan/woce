The Carbon Book app makes it easy to track, reduce and remove CO2 emissions from everyday life. A non-profit social initiative for individuals to start tracking their carbon footprints.

 

Individuals can capture their day to day activities under multiple categories. Our year long researched algorithms calculate carbon emissions based on individual activities. All our algorithms are complaint with government and international standards.
