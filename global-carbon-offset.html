EKI Energy Services Ltd. (EKI), a leading developer and supplier of carbon credits across the globe. Together with WOCE, it aims to facilitate individuals, businesses, and organizations with a one-stop solution for all aspects of carbon inventory management and neutrality/net-zero solutions.

WOCE & EKI Energy Services partner to provide sustainable solutions to businesses. Press release link.

EKI provides 15% of the voluntary carbon credit market share globally (as of 2021). With over 14 years of global experience in offset solutions, we offer complete end-to-end management of carbon assets, empowering organizations to achieve their net-zero goals. With expertise in Gold Standard, Verified Carbon Standards (VCS), Clean Development Mechanism (CDM), VERRA, SD VISTa, Global Carbon Council (GCC), CERCARBONO and Puro Standard amongst others, we enable organizations to understand climate nuances seamlessly and earn high quality credits for their projects.
