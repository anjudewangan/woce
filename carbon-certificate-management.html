Carbon Certificate Management (CCM) platform is designed to help organizations manage the lifecycle of carbon credits (compliance & voluntary). This platform enables enterprises to accurately track and reconcile the carbon credit inventories against carbon registries and compliance registries.

CCM platform can be easily integrated with Trade Capture system for carbon credit valuation & to reconcile the inventory with carbon exchanges. Further it can be integrated with enterprise’s emission tracking system or Carbon Ledger Platform to ensure correct marking of credits which are being retired to offset organization emissions.

WHY

Importance of Carbon Certificate Mgmt.

Organizations are using excel spreadsheets to track their carbon credit inventory which is error prone and lacks auditing.

It is extremely cumbersome and manual effort to reconcile the voluntary carbon credit inventories across various carbon registries.

Often custom solutions are built, or trading systems are customized to manage the inventory and valuation which is tedious to manage and support in long run.

Carbon pricing for voluntary credits varies across carbon registries and by geography & type of the carbon credit project and can be traded globally, so requires different analytics to provide insights to carbon traders compared to compliance markets.

FEATURES

Key Features for Enterprises

One stop shop to view inventory compliance and voluntary carbon credits across geo’s & carbon registries.

Auto reconciliation of carbon inventories against carbon registries.

Supports easy integration with Trading System for valuation and exchange carbon position reconciliation.

Supports easy integration with enterprise emissions tracking platform or Carbon Ledger platform.

Detailed Insights and Analytics to build your carbon trading strategies. E.g., make profit by selling excess of carbon credits or buy carbon credits at lower prices to offset your organization’s emissions.

BENEFITS

Key Benefits for Enterprises

Provides Carbon traders with an insight into real time inventory of carbon credits for taking trading decisions real time during volatile carbon market.

Provides a system of record across enterprise for carbon credit inventory.

Eliminates manual effort of carbon credit inventory reconciliation.

Creates additional revenue stream by enabling buying & selling of carbon credits.

Ensures transparency & audit trail for each credit being retired to offset organization’s emission or for each credit being bought or sold in the carbon market.
