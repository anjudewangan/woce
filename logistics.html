Solution Offerings

Our Solutions for Logistics companies include carbon accounting of scope1, scope 2 and scope 3 emissions. These carbon accounting solutions include capturing of various activities across Logistics value chain and computing of emissions at every step in value chain using our industry vetted algorithms. Carbon offset management solution(COMS) help logistics companies to manage their carbon offset projects and carbon certificates across various registries

Carbon Accounting

Capture Scope 1, Scope 2 and Scope 3 emission along different logistics modes like road, rail, marine, airways and pipelines. Identify various business activities across the value chain and measure carbon emissions along the value chain

Carbon Offset Strategies

Emission calculation on every movement of product enables forecasting of carbon emission across portfolios and business units which then can enable organizations to plan carbon offset strategies in advance by purchasing carbon credits at better prices

Carbon Offset Management

Carbon Offset Management System (COMS) enables organizations to manage & reconcile carbon certificates between registries (compliance & voluntary markets) and Trade Capture systems on one unified carbon intelligence platform
