AI and Sustainability a powerful combo

esgpro.ai, a state-of-the-art ESG platform leverages advanced AI capabilities to provide a comprehensive and impactful solution for managing environmental, social, and governance data and setting and achieving net zero ambition for businesses of all sizes.

In today’s rapidly evolving business landscape, sustainability and responsible governance have become critical to long-term success. esgpro.ai stands at the forefront of this transformation, offering a cutting-edge ESG platform that harnesses the power of artificial intelligence (AI) to deliver a comprehensive and impactful solution for managing environmental, social, and governance data.

Key Features

AI-Powered ESG Data Capture

Offers robust ESG data capture capabilities, utilizing advanced AI to ensure accurate and efficient data collection. It supports a broad range of global frameworks – GRI, CDP, SASB, ISSB, IFRS, BRSR, CBAM, PCAF.

Industry Benchmarking on ESG Parameters

Gain insights into how your organization measures up against industry standards with benchmarking tools that compare ESG performance with industry peers, helping you identify areas for improvement to stay competitive.

Activity Data Integration with ERP Systems

Seamlessly integrate activity data from your ERP systems to streamline data flow and ensure that all relevant ESG data is captured accurately and efficiently, minimizing manual entry and reducing errors.

Complete Insights and Analytics of ESG & GHG Accounting Data

Gain comprehensive insights and analytics on your ESG and GHG accounting data with detailed dashboards and reports, enabling you to analyse performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

Marketplace for Carbon Credits

Easily purchase carbon credits through an integrated marketplace which offers access to a variety of projects, including renewables, community projects, and I-RECs, ensuring you can find industry best prices and projects that align with your sustainability goals.

Data Validation & Verification Workflows

Maintain the integrity of your ESG data with built-in validation and verification workflows using AI , ensuring that all data entered into the system is checked for accuracy and consistency to provide a reliable foundation for reporting and analysis.

Carbon Accounting as per GHG Protocol or ISO Protocol

The platform supports carbon accounting in line with globally recognized standards that ensures your carbon footprint calculations are precise, reliable, and compliant with international best practices.

Predictive Analytics for Emissions & Net Zero Targets

Utilize predictive analytics to forecast emissions and track progress towards net-zero targets by leveraging AI to provide forward-looking insights, helping you anticipate challenges and make informed decisions.

Digitize Decarbonization Roadmap & Net Zero Goal Tracking

Plan and track your journey to net-zero emissions with digital tools designed for decarbonization including a comprehensive decarbonization roadmap and goal-tracking features, helping you set and achieve your net-zero targets as per Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi).

Configurable Emission Sources, Emission Factors, and Master Data

Tailor the platform to your specific needs with customization based on industry sectors.
